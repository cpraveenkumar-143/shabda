SHABDA

Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025

Shabda does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Ever.

Overview

Shabda is a language learning mobile application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Shabda to learn Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, or Bahasa Indonesia.

Data We Do Not Collect

Shabda does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Shabda stores the following data locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Your lesson progress (which lessons you have completed)
  • Your XP (experience points) total
  • Your daily streak count
  • Your selected language preferences

This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Text-to-Speech

Shabda uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine to play audio pronunciations for language lessons. This is a native operating system feature. No audio data is recorded or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Shabda does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Shabda is suitable for users of all ages including children under 13. Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special data handling requirements under COPPA, GDPR-K, or similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
