# Privacy Policy — Shabda

**Last updated: March 2025**

## Overview

Shabda ("the app") is a language learning mobile application developed by cpraveenkumar-143. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Shabda.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Shabda does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying information
- We do not create user accounts
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- We do not transmit any data to external servers

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Shabda stores the following data **locally on your device only** using AsyncStorage:

- Your lesson progress (which lessons you have completed)
- Your XP (experience points) total
- Your daily streak count
- Your selected language preferences

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

Shabda uses the device's built-in **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** engine to play audio for language lessons. This is a native OS feature and does not send data to any third party.

Shabda does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

Shabda is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, there are no special considerations required under COPPA or similar regulations.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the updated version here with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
